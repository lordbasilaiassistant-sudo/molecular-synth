Water module — a clean glass of water in seconds

3D sim of the Molecular Synth water maker. On demand it valve-dispenses from a treated, pre-chilled buffer (carbon-filtered + UV-C dosed), filling a 250 mL glass in seconds. The buffer is kept filled from a tap/reservoir (instant) or, off-grid, harvested slowly from air by the Peltier AWG — so we never depend on humidity: dispense = seconds either way.
